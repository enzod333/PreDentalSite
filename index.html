About Us

The Pre-Dental Society at SFSU strives to serve as a resource for students who are interested in pursuing dentistry as a professional career. As a club, we provide opportunities on community outreach, shadowing programs, Kaplan workshops, and dental education.

Past and Upcoming Events

  • October 23rd, 2015

    Oral Hygiene Education

    Grades Kindergarten through 5 at Lakeshore Elementary School were educated on the importance of Oral Hygiene. Topics included basic information about brushing, flossing, and visiting the dentist.

  • November 17th, 2015

    UCSF Dental Student Panel

    A panel of 3 dental students answered questions and gave valuable informal advice to our members. There was also much information discussed regarding the application and interview processes and what to expect in Dental School.

  • Throughout the Month of February and March 2016

    National Oral Health Foundation Smile Drive

    Members will be visiting various Dental Offices around the surrounding area to gather donations for the charity of our choice. Donations include toothbrushes, toothpaste, dental floss, mouthwash, and other oral hygiene items.

  • February 20th, 2016

    UCSF Intro to Dental Day

    PDS members please join us for a tour of UCSF to see the campus, meet faculty, get advising, and learn more about the admission process to UCSF. Sign up HERE!

  • February 23rd, 2016

    Presentation by Dr. Roger Mraz

    A presentation will be given by Fr. Roger Mraz who teaches at UCSF. More information to come. Stay tuned!

  • April 9th, 2016

    UOP Pride Day

    PDS members please join us for a tour of Unvirsity of the Pacific Dental School to see the campus, meet faculty, get advising, and learn more about the admission process to UOP. More Information to come!
